Yoté Board and Elephants

18 Oct 2018

I used to work for a company that hosted annual employee art shows. This was a great way to share creative passions with coworkers and I participated one year with a wall full (10-12) of elephant paintings.

I’ve been looking for a way to incorporate elephants into one of my board games and making a Yoté board was the perfect excuse (as well as it being a fun game!). Wood burning on red oak can be a little tricky but I was pleased with the results.
