Print On Demand Gems

30 Apr 2021

Print on demand games can get a bad rap. I think this is mostly because anyone with an idea can publish a game regardless if it’s been vetted or not. This is not necessarily a bad thing. It gives opportunity to those who may not have another avenue for bringing their games to life. In the age of self-published content, there is a lot that needs to be filtered. Here is a couple of print on demand games from several print companies that I think are worth taking a look at (though not all of them are abstract strategy games).

Print and Play Games
Ni-Ju by Néstor Romeral Andrés
Blue by Néstor Romeral Andrés

The Game Crafter
Four Tribes by Grey Gnome Games
Memory Kings by Sneaky Pirate Games
Pai Sho by The Garden Gate
Camelot by Parker Brothers
Cam by Parker Brothers

Drive Thru Cards
Pairs: Tree Deck by Cheapass Games
Light Speed by Cheapass Games
