Many Chess Tutorials with Triple S Games

20 Feb 2022

It’s no secret that I LOVE chess variants. So I was pleasantly surprised to discover the Triple S Games YouTube channel where there are many succinct tutorials on exactly that. The channel is not exclusive to chess variants, but has many abstract tutorials as well. The videos are short and sweet and high quality. Here is a sample of some that caught my eye:

Puzzle Board Chess
Los Alamos Chess
Chess Unbound
Janus Chess
Diplomat Chess
Glinski’s Hexagonal Chess
Infinite Chess

In addition, Triple S Games also has many custom components on The Game Crafter. For those of you who don’t have the exotic board sizes or pieces needed to play some of these variants, check it out!
