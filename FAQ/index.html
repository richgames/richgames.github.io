I want to know more about hnefatafl! Where should I go look?

Hnefatafl - the Game of the Vikings is a rich source of all things hnefatafl. Also, Damian Walker's books are available on Lulu.com for purchase.

What is the criteria when writing a Modern Abstract Spotlight?

It's pretty informal. but here is a short list that guides it:

1. The game is created after 1900.
2. There is a print and play publicly available.
3. I've played the game at least 10 times and still enjoy it!
