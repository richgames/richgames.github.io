ChatGPT Writes Me a Hnefatafl Blog

09 May 2023

After months of hearing about all the ChatGPT hype, I finally bit the bullet and gave the AI a spin. I asked ChatGPT to write a blog about Hnefatafl and I was pretty impressed by what it spit out in a matter of seconds.

NOTE: The exact prompt I used was “Write me a blog about hnefatafl.”

What does this mean?? Yay, my blog writing just got easier! Just kidding. Although, I wouldn’t use ChatGPT to replace my writing (mostly because I like the process of writing). I can see how it can be used as a tool for starting articles and brainstorming. In the coming years, it will be interesting to see how/if the world will embrace to use of AI in everyday life.
