Abstract Tutorials and Reviews with The Boardgame Kaptain

09 Jan 2022

It is easy to get lost in the ever expanding sea of channels on YouTube. With the explosion of game tutorials and reviews, everyone is vying for clicks and views. But one in particular I wanted to bring attention to is the The Boardgame Kaptain. Although it is not solely for abstract or historical games, I am surprised by the amount of abstract games this channel contains. I did a quick peruse of the uploaded videos and thought I’d share a list:

NXS Ragnarok
Quantum
Game of Y
Batalo
Mijnlieff
Mijnlieff Modular Board Expansion
Hexentafl
Paco Sako
Raft & Scupper
Sovereign Chess
Saiju
Flink
Onager
Niju
KuniUmi
Green Skull
Return to Camelot
Shobu
Royal Game of Ur

This is not an exhaustive list of the abstracts on their channel but it’s already an impressive one! It’s obvious to me that The Boardgame Kaptain likes to highlight small indie developers of abstract games and everyone who is interested should go check out his channel!
